Overview

This dashboard monitors for slow-onset crises across ROSEA countries without a permanent OCHA presence. Each country is assigned an alert level indicating the possible severity of a crisis, ranging from low to very high. Alert levels are assigned based on thresholds applied to the latest available IPC food security data and JRC Anomaly Hotspots of Agricultural Production (ASAP).

This application is an internal tool under development. For any enquiries please contact the OCHA Centre for Humanitarian Data at ocha-datascience@un.org. Sign up for HDX Signals for additional email alerts on IPC and ASAP data.
